Simple to Set Up. Easy to Use.

Follow these steps for a perfect fit every time.

HEAD COLLAR MODE (For More Control)
  1. 1 Loop the rope over your dog's nose (not too tight — two fingers should fit).
  2. 2 Slide the stopper up behind the ears to secure the fit.
  3. 3 Attach the safety clip to your dog's collar or harness.
  4. 4 Hold the leash with a relaxed grip — let the nose pressure do the work.
  5. 5 Reward calm walking. Release tension as soon as your dog relaxes.
SLIP LEAD MODE (Quick & Simple)
  1. 1 Slip the loop over your dog's head and let it sit just behind the ears.
  2. 2 Adjust the stopper so the loop is snug but comfortable.
  3. 3 Walk with a loose leash — the stopper tightens only if your dog pulls.

Don't hold tight — keep the leash relaxed and let the Sidekick do the work

Don't skip the safety clip — it keeps your dog secure if the nose loop slips
