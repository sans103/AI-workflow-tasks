Understanding AI

What AI Can Do

  • Process and summarize large amounts of data
  • Automate repetitive tasks
  • Generate drafts, ideas, and variations
  • Analyze patterns and provide insights
  • Work 24/7 without fatigue

What AI Cannot Do

  • Replace human judgment and creativity
  • Understand context without guidance
  • Take accountability for decisions
  • Build genuine client relationships
  • Guarantee 100% accuracy
