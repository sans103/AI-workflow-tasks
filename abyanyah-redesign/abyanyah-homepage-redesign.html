The Founder

“I built A by Anyah as a movement — a rebellion against the ordinary. Every bag is designed to make you feel protected, powerful, and unapologetically yourself.”

Anyah transformed personal adversity into creative strength, founding a brand that celebrates feminine power and sisterhood. Surrounded by strong, fearless, and beautiful women, she designs pieces that are as bold as the women who carry them.
