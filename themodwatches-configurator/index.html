WHAT'S INSIDE YOUR KIT

All Components Arranged — Photo Placeholder
The heart of your watch. The Seiko NH70 is a self-winding skeleton movement with a 41-hour power reserve. Visible from both sides through the sapphire crystal caseback. Hacking seconds hand for precise time-setting.
316L stainless steel case with brushed and polished finish. 42mm diameter, 13mm thickness. Screw-down crown and caseback. 100m water resistance rating.
Your choice of 3 bezels from 6 options. Ceramic inserts with lume-filled markers. Snap-fit design for easy swapping. Sub-style, GMT, and Yacht-Master designs available.
Choose 2 dials: Skeleton Black and Blue let you see the movement in action. Sub-Style Black gives you a classic dive watch face with applied markers and SuperLuminova.
One stainless steel bracelet (three-link or five-link), plus 2 additional straps from NATO nylon, rubber, or wild leather options. Quick-release spring bars for tool-free strap changes.
Double-domed sapphire crystal with anti-reflective coating. Scratch-resistant (9 on the Mohs scale). The same material used in watches costing thousands.
Spring bar tool, case opener, movement holder, screwdriver set, dust blower, and microfiber cloth. Everything you need to assemble and maintain your watch.
Your kit arrives in a presentation-grade box with individual compartments for each component. Designed for the unboxing experience to feel as premium as the build itself.
