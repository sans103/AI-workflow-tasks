The Craft of Pure Linen

From European Flax to Your Wardrobe

Every Linen Trail shirt begins as premium European flax — selected for fiber length, strength, and natural luster. What you wear is the result of centuries of craft, refined for modern life.

Sourced With Intention

Our 60 LEA pure linen is sourced from European flax fields where the cool climate produces the longest, strongest fibers. Each batch is tested for tensile strength and natural luster before weaving.

Zero Shrinkage, Guaranteed

Our proprietary pre-shrinkage treatment ensures the fabric you buy is the fabric you keep — for years. No surprises after the first wash. The dimensions remain true, wash after wash.

Nature's Performance Fabric

Naturally breathable, antibacterial, and hypoallergenic. Linen regulates body temperature 3x better than cotton — keeping you cool in heat and comfortable through the day. And it only gets softer with every wash.
