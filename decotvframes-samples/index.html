FAQ
Common questions
Everything you need to know before you order.
What frame styles are available?
Between our Deco Premiere and Deco Alloy Collections, there are 40+ distinct styles — from silver and gold metallics to warm natural wood finishes. Deco Premiere starts at $299; Deco Alloy bezels start at $199.
What TVs are these frames compatible with?
Made exclusively for Samsung The Frame TV and Samsung The Frame Pro. Available for 32", 43", 50", 55", 65", 75", and 85". Always match your frame size to your TV's exact size.
How do I install a Deco TV Frame?
Under 2 minutes, no tools needed. Assemble the frame with the magnetic corner connectors, hook onto the top of your Frame TV, clip into place at the bottom. The magnetic system holds securely.
How do I know what a frame looks like in my room?
Order a sample first — the most reliable way to see the exact finish under your lighting. We also have a visualization tool: point your camera at your wall and see a scaled virtual frame in your space.
Is digital art included with Samsung The Frame?
Yes — Samsung The Frame comes with 33 pre-loaded art images. Create a Deco TV Frames account and get instant access to 500 additional digital art images as well.
Do samples earn credit toward a full frame?
Yes. Every dollar spent on samples earns a dollar-for-dollar credit toward a full frame — up to $25. Auto-applied at checkout. Shipping excluded.
