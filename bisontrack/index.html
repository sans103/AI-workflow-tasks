Our Story

Family-Owned. Rider-Founded. Built for The Herd.

Bison Track was born when co-founder Rob Lackey — a passionate motorcycle racer — crashed in an affordable but low-quality custom suit and realized riders deserved better. Together with his wife Tosha, a CPA and marketing strategist, they set out to build pro-level bespoke gear at a price every rider can afford.

Today, the Lackeys travel full-time in their RV to racetracks across North America, fitting riders in person and growing "The Herd" — a community united by a shared love of riding and the belief that your gear should be a reflection of you. That philosophy has a name: BISONALITY.

Frequently Asked Questions

We know there are a lot of great companies out there who can get you in a suit. We also know that there is no other company who offers a one-on-one experience catered just for you, at the price we do, and certainly not while providing the pro-level quality you'll find in your Bison gear. The bottom line is this: You're treated like a pro no matter who you are, and your gear will be built with the same care and materials whether you're riding on the street or on the television. We're also a family-owned business with a true passion for motorcycle road racing.
The great thing about a Bison kit is that it is built just for you, from measurements to colors and logos. Once you place your order on our website, you will receive an order confirmation with important next steps. We work one-on-one with you to design the suit and gloves of your dreams. Due to demand, our team is unable to work on designs without a paid order in our system.
The design process can be completed in as quickly as 24 hours, however it isn't unusual for us to spend time with the customer tweaking and modifying designs. Our average design time is about two weeks. Once the design is approved and we have measurements, completed suit and/or gloves are typically in your hands in under 60 days!
